Lace lingerie is beautiful but delicate. Washing it incorrectly can cause it to tear, lose its shape, or fade. Here is how to keep your lace looking new for longer.

## Hand Washing is Best
Always hand wash your lace lingerie whenever possible.
1. Fill a sink with lukewarm water.
2. Add a mild detergent specifically designed for delicates.
3. Swish the water to create suds.
4. Submerge your lingerie and let it soak for 15-20 minutes.
5. Gently rub any stained areas with your fingers.
6. Rinse thoroughly with cool water.

## Machine Washing (If You Must)
If you're pressed for time, you can machine wash your lace, but proceed with caution.
- Always use a **mesh laundry bag** to prevent snagging.
- Select the **delicate cycle** with cold water.
- Avoid using bleach or fabric softener.

## Drying
Never put lace in the dryer. The heat can break down the fibers and elastic. Instead:
- Lay your lingerie flat on a clean towel.
- Roll the towel to absorb excess water.
- Unroll and lay flat to dry completely, away from direct sunlight.

## Conclusion
With a little extra care, your lace lingerie will stay beautiful and comfortable for years to come.
