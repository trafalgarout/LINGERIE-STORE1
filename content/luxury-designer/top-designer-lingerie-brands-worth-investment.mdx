## How to Care for Your Investment Pieces

To ensure your **luxury lingerie designers**' pieces last a lifetime, follow these golden rules:

1.  **Rotate Your Bras:** Never wear the same bra two days in a row. The elastic needs 24 hours to recover its shape.
2.  **Use a Lingerie Wash bag:** If you must machine wash (only on delicate!), use a mesh bag to prevent snags.
3.  **Lay Flat to Dry:** Never, ever put luxury lingerie in the dryer. Heat destroys Lycra and silk.
