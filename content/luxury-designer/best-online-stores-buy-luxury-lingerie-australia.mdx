### 1. The GST Threshold
As of recent regulations, goods imported into Australia valued at AUD $1,000 or less are subject to 10% GST at checkout. Many international **luxury lingerie ecommerce** platforms now collect this automatically, so there are no surprise charges on delivery.

### 2. Australian Sizing
Australian bra sizing follows the British/European system but with some quirks. An Australian 10C is equivalent to a UK 10C or US 32C. Most **designer lingerie online shop** platforms default to US or European sizes, so always check the conversion chart.

### 3. Return Logistics
Returning lingerie from Australia to an overseas warehouse can cost $30-$60 AUD. Prioritize stores with:
*   **Free returns** (rare for international, common for Australian retailers).
*   **Australian return addresses** (some global brands have local return points).
*   **Prepaid return labels** included in the package.

## Shopping Guide: Tips for Australian Buyers

### 1. Time Your Purchases
The best sales in Australian luxury lingerie happen during:
*   **Boxing Day / End of Year:** Honey Birdette and The Iconic run their biggest sales.

When the AUD is strong against the EUR or GBP, buying directly from European brands can be significantly cheaper than buying through Australian retailers.
