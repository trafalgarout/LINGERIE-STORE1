## Buying Guide: Identifying Quality Lace

Not all lace is created equal. Here is how to spot **premium lace lingerie**:

1.  **The Eyelash Edge:** High-quality French lace often has delicate "eyelashes" at the hem, which must be cut by hand.
2.  **Softness Test:** Rub the lace against your inner wrist. If it scratches, it’s likely unmatched nylon. True luxury lace feels like silk.
3.  **Pattern Alignment:** On **designer lace bra and panty sets**, the pattern should mirror perfectly on both cups.

## FAQ: Lace Lingerie Care & Fit

**Q: Is lace lingerie itchy?**
A: Cheap lace can be. However, **luxury lace lingerie sets** use high-thread-count materials and often line the cups with tulle or silk to ensure softness against the skin.

**Q: Can I wear lace bras under T-shirts?**
A: Textured lace (like Guipure) may show through tight tops. For a smooth silhouette, look for "flat lace" or stretch Leavers lace.

**Q: How do I wash my lace sets?**
A: Always hand wash in cool water with a dedicated lingerie detergent. Never wring out the water; press it out gently with a towel.
