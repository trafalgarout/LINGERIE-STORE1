### 1. The EU Advantage
As a member of the European Union, Sweden benefits from free movement of goods within the single market. This means:
*   **No customs duties** on orders from EU-based retailers (France, Italy, Belgium, Germany, etc.).
*   **No surprise fees** at delivery for intra-EU purchases.
*   **VAT included:** Most EU retailers display prices inclusive of Swedish MOMS (25%).

This makes buying French and Italian luxury lingerie significantly easier and cheaper for Swedes than for shoppers in the US, UK, or Australia.

### 2. Non-EU Orders
For purchases from the UK (post-Brexit), US, or Asian retailers:
*   **Customs duty:** Up to 12% on textile goods.
*   **Swedish MOMS:** 25% VAT on the total value (goods + shipping + duty).
*   **Threshold:** There is no duty-free threshold in Sweden since 2021—all imports are subject to VAT.

### 3. Swedish Sizing
Sweden uses the European sizing system (70A, 75B, 80C, etc.). The number refers to the underbust measurement in centimeters, and the letter indicates the cup size. Most international **luxury lingerie ecommerce** platforms include EU sizing options.

## FAQ: Buying Luxury Lingerie in Sweden

**Q: Do I pay customs on UK lingerie brands after Brexit?**
A: Yes. Since Brexit, UK-to-Sweden orders are treated as non-EU imports. Expect customs duty (up to 12%) plus 25% MOMS. However, many UK brands now ship from EU warehouses to avoid these charges—always check the shipping origin.

**Q: What is the best store for French lingerie in Sweden?**
A: **NK** stocks Aubade and La Perla in-store. **Boozt** carries Simone Pérèle and Chantelle online. For the widest French selection, **NET-A-PORTER** is the strongest option, shipping duty-free from within the EU.

**Q: Is Swedish sizing the same as European?**
A: Yes. Swedish bra sizing uses the European system (70, 75, 80, etc. for band; A, B, C, etc. for cup). A Swedish 75C is the same as a European 75C, a UK 34C, or a US 34C.

**Q: Can I return worn lingerie?**
A: Swedish consumer law (Distansavtalslagen) allows a 14-day return period for online purchases. However, hygiene items (lingerie) can only be returned if the hygiene strip is intact and the item is unworn. Most retailers extend this to 28-30 days.
