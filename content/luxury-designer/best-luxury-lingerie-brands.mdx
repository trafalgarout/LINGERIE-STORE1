## 1. La Perla: The Epitome of Italian Luxury
If there is one name synonymous with **premium lingerie brands for women**, it is La Perla. Founded in 1954 in Bologna, Ada Masotti began crafting corsetry that would set the global standard for luxury.

### Why We Love It
La Perla is famous for its **Maison Collection**—featuring hand-embroidered Frastaglio lace on silk satin. It is sophisticated enough for daily wear but luxurious enough for special occasions.

**Key Pieces:**
*   Silk Pajama Sets
*   Maison Slips
*   Petit Macramé Bras

> **Expert Tip:** La Perla runs true to size. Their silk pieces have no stretch, so measure yourself accurately before purchasing.

## Buying Guide: How to Choose the Perfect Luxury Set

Purchasing from **luxury lingerie brands for women** can be daunting. Here is what to look for:

### 1. Fabric Quality
Always check the composition label.
*   **Silk:** Breathable, hypoallergenic, and smooth.
*   **Polyester Satin:** Often cheaper brands use this. It can trap heat.
*   **Modal:** Great for everyday luxury comfort.

### 2. Construction Details
Turn the piece inside out. **Premium lingerie** will have:
*   Enclosed seams (French seams) so no raw edges touch your skin.
*   Silk-wrapped straps.
*   Gold or silver-plated hardware instead of plastic.

### 3. Fit & Sizing
Luxury brands often use European sizing (EU 70C vs US 32C). Always refer to the brand’s specific size chart. Don’t assume your Victoria's Secret size applies here.

## Care & Maintenance for Designer Lingerie

To protect your investment in **expensive lingerie brands**, follow these rules:

1.  **Hand Wash Only:** Never put silk or lace in the machine. Use a gentle detergent like Soak or Eucalan.
2.  **Dry Flat:** Hanging wet silk can stretch the fibers. Lay flat on a clean towel.
3.  **Store Properly:** Keep bras stacked to maintain cup shape. Use satin bags for delicate lace sets.
